Chicken Kiev

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 100g (3.5 oz) unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1½ cups breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Make garlic butter: Mix softened butter with garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Shape into a log, wrap in foil, and freeze until firm.
  2. Prepare chicken: Pound chicken breasts flat. Place a piece of frozen garlic butter in the center, fold and seal tightly.
  3. Bread the chicken: Dredge in flour, dip in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  4. Chill: Refrigerate for 30 minutes to help hold shape.
  5. Cook: Deep-fry or pan-fry until golden and cooked through (about 8–10 minutes). Finish in oven if needed.

Serve hot with mashed potatoes or a crisp salad. Enjoy that buttery explosion!
