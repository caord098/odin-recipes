Draniki

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Peel and finely grate the potatoes and onion.
  2. Squeeze out excess liquid from the mixture.
  3. Add the egg, flour, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Spoon the mixture into small pancakes and flatten slightly.
  6. Fry until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve hot with sour cream or fresh herbs.
