Cheesy Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tortillas
  • 200g cooked chicken, sliced
  • 100g shredded mozzarella
  • 50g shredded cheddar
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp garlic sauce or mayo
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Warm the tortillas slightly.
  2. Spread garlic sauce or mayo on each tortilla.
  3. Add chicken, tomato, onion, and both cheeses.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Roll tightly into wraps.
  6. Grill in a pan until golden and cheese melts.
  7. Slice and serve warm.
